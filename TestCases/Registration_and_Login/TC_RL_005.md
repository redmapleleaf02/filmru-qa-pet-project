# filmru – Test Case 5

## ID: TC-RL-005

## Title: Registration attempt with invalid email format

### Preconditions:
- Website is open on homepage;
- User is not registered yet.

### Steps:
1. Click on the “Войти” (Login) button to open the combined login/registration form;
2. Enter an email without “@” or domain (e.g., “testmail”).

### Expected Result:
- Error message is displayed: “Неверные данные”.
