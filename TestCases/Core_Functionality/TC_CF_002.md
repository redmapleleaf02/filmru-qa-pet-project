# filmru – Test Case 2

## ID: TC-CF-002

## Title: Delete account

### Preconditions:
- User is logged in.

### Steps:
1. Click on the profile icon;
2. Click on the “Настройки” (Settings) button;
3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page;
4. Click on the “Delete account” (Удалить аккаунт) button. 

### Expected Result:
- A window titled "Удаление аккаунта" (Account Deletion) appears with a field where user can indicate the reason for deletion and “Удалить” (Delete) button at the bottom.
