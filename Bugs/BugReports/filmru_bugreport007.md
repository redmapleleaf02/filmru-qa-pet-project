# filmru – Bug Report №7

## ID: FBR-007

## Title:
- Sidebar – “О персоне” section does not respond to click

## Description:
- On actor/celebrity information pages, the sidebar contains sections: “О персоне”, “Фото”, “В центре внимания”, “Лучшие фильмы и сериалы”, “Фильмография”, “Публикации”, “Новости”. Clicking on all sections except “О персоне” leads to chosen sections. However, clicking on “О персоне” produces no effect, and the user remains on the same section without any response.

## Environment:
- OS: Windows 11;
- Browser: Microsoft Edge (version 145.0.3800.70).

## Steps:
1. Open the “film.ru” website;
2. Enter the name of any actor/celebrity in the search bar and select the first suggestion in the “Люди” (People) section;
3. In the sidebar, click on the “О персоне” (About person) section;
4. Observe the behavior compared to other sections.

## Expected Result:
- Clicking on “О персоне” navigates to the section content about person.

## Actual Result:
- Clicking on “О персоне” produces no effect. The section does not respond, unlike other sidebar sections.

## Severity:
- Low.

## Priority:
- Minor.
