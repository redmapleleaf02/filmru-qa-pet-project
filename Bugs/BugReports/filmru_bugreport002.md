# filmru – Bug Report №2

## ID: FBR-002

## Title:
- Absence of scroll-bar on sidebar while navigating on homepage

## Description:
- Moving the cursor over the sidebar buttons make them longer and it makes a user to scroll down all over the homepage to see and click the rest of sidebar buttons.

## Environment:
- OS: Windows 11;
- Browser: Microsoft Edge (version 145.0.3800.70).

## Steps:
1. Open the “film.ru” website;
2. Move the cursor over the sidebar buttons so all their contents will be shown;
3. Start to scroll down to click the lowest button on the sidebar.

## Expected Result:
- Scroll-bar shows up on the sidebar. User can click the lowest button easily.

## Actual Result:
- There is no scroll-bar on the sidebar. User has to scroll down all over the homepage to see and click the rest of sidebar buttons.

## Severity:
- Low.

## Priority:
- Minor.
