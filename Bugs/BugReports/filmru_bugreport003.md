# filmru – Bug Report №3

## ID: FBR-003

## Title:
- Absence of scroll-bar on hamburger menu while navigating on homepage

## Description:
- Hamburger menu has 8 different sections and the last 2 of them are hidden at the bottom of hamburger menu. User has to scroll down all over the homepage to see and click the last 2 sections.

## Environment:
- OS: Windows 11;
- Browser: Microsoft Edge (version 145.0.3800.70).

## Steps:
1. Open the “film.ru” website;
2. Click on the hamburger menu;
3. Start to scroll down to see and click the rest 2 sections.

## Expected Result:
- Scroll-bar shows up on the hamburger menu, independent of the homepage scroll. User can see and click the rest 2 sections easily.

## Actual Result:
- There is no scroll-bar on the hamburger menu. User has to scroll down all over the homepage to see and click the rest 2 sections.

## Severity:
- Low.

## Priority:
- Minor.
